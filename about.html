About InTown

In the beginning...

InTown was originally idealized in 2016 by Sheldon in his college dorm room. Sheldon was looking for a way to incorporate the skills he was learning in college to make an impact on the community. He conducted research on areas of business where the most impact could be made. He found that during the time technology and the internet was becoming an increasingly important part of business and every day life. He found that in 2016 many businesses had no web presence. Little did he know his observation was going to become even more important with the pandemic coming in 2020. Information Technology would be needed in business now more than ever. To his benefit he could create websites and understand new technology with ease so he found that would be a great place to start. Sheldon created the name InTown as a catchy play on words that would emphasis the new world Information Technology was bringing into the business world.

Growing Changes...

Through the years InTown has experienced growth and developed but the company has battled a few issues caused by the “haters”. With the help of some strong mentors and determination of Sheldon to continue on his path InTown has forged two new business identities MMM and InTown VIP. These organizations were created to support ITs mission. MMM essentially a holding company to add organizational structure and InTown VIP to make the brand more exclusive. The future of InTown is bright the company continues to grow on Sheldon’s accurate predictions of the future. Sheldon gives special thanks to the leaders that support InTown and wants to see IT succeed. InTown is executively managed by MMM.
